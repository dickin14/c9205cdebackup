Welcome to FFXIV Beginners!
Ever thought about getting started with Final Fantasy XIV? Ever wanted to know a little more about the basics of the game? Well, maybe we can help.
Here we'll give you a little overview of the different classes you can play in the game, in addition to a little bit of insight into the starting cities.
Click on the 'Classes' button below, or go straight to looking at some of the cities using the navigation bar at the top.

But what are classes?

'Classes' are the starting roles your character will play. While creating your character, you will be asked to select one of the Disciples of War, or Disciples of Magic.
The class you choose determines the abilities your character has, and the equipment that they can use.
Don't worry if you don't like your initial choice, you can change later!

Starting cities?

There are three major cities in the world of Eorzea; Gridania, Ul'dah, and Limsa Lominsa.
The class you choose at the start of the game determines the city in which you begin your adventure, and the beginning narrative you follow.
Once the beginning of the plot is established however, the plots from all three cities converge, and you get the same experience as everyone else, so don't worry too much about your choice!
